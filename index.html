Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Focuspace is designed with a simple principle: your focus time is yours alone. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. Focuspace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry.

Local data

The app stores the following on your device only, never leaving it:

  • Your world state (tree growth progress, current season, meditation minutes)
  • Your mixer preferences (which ambient sounds are active and their volumes)
  • Your hotkey and appearance preferences

These are saved via macOS UserDefaults and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

Network access

Focuspace does not connect to the internet. All ambient sounds, fonts, and assets are bundled inside the app.

Third-party services

None. Focuspace does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children

Focuspace is safe for all ages. Because no data is collected, no special provisions are needed for users under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect it. Since the app does not phone home, any changes will only apply to future versions you choose to install.

Support

How do I toggle between interactive mode and wallpaper mode?

Press ⌘ + ⇧ + M (Cmd + Shift + M). In wallpaper mode the app becomes click-through so you can interact with windows behind it. Press again to bring the control panel back.

Where is the app? I can't find it in the Dock.

Focuspace is a menu bar app — it lives in the top-right of your screen, not the Dock. Look for the small leaf icon. Right-click for options including Quit.

How do I quit Focuspace?

Right-click the menu bar icon and choose Quit.

My tree isn't growing. What's wrong?

Trees grow as meditation minutes accumulate. Start a session from the onboarding screen or the Personal Center. Growth is gradual — a tree takes many sessions to mature.

Can I reset my world?

Yes. Open Personal Center from the menu bar icon and choose Reset. This cannot be undone.
